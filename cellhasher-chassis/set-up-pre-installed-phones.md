# Set-Up (Pre-Installed Phones)

***

**Before powering on your Cellhasher**, follow this guide on how to inspect the input voltage to avoid electrical stress on your unit as well as ensure your phones are connected.

## Cellhasher Classic +20 Phones

#### Inspect Input Voltage

1. Determine if the circuit associated to the outlet you plan to connect to is rated for 120V or 240V. This is important to confirm.
2. Every Cellhasher has an adjustable power supply unit (PSU) that supports either 120V or 240V. You will want to confirm it is set to your corresponding circuit. To toggle between these, locate the 120V-240V yellow sticker next to the red slider in the PSU, underneath phone slots 14 and 15. Use a long, rigid tool (such as a screw driver) and fully move the red slider according to your circuit, if it is not already.

#### Confirm Solid Connections

If your Cellhasher is on and a phone hasn't powered on, shipment can result in disconnected or loose connections. Visually inspect both ports for each phone and apply a gentle push to each connection to ensure it's properly in place.&#x20;

#### Powering On

You can now safely plug-in and power on your Cellhasher. The Cellhasher can take up to 1 minute to fully power on all phones.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Tip:** It is normal and expected in some circumstances if not all phones turn on automatically when powering on the _**Cellhasher Classic +20 Phones**_. To resolve, short-press the relevant phone's power button. If the phone remains off, long-press the power button until turned on.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="success" %}
That's it! Proceed to downloading Cellhasher Control in [set-up-download.md](../cellhasher-control/set-up-download.md "mention")
{% endhint %}

## Cellhasher Rackmount +20 Phones

#### Inspect Input Voltage

The power supply installed supports and automatically detects whether the circuit is 120V or 240V. No action is required.

#### Confirm Solid Connections

If your Cellhasher is on and a phone hasn't powered on, shipment can result in disconnected or loose connections. Visually inspect both ports for each phone and apply a gentle push to each connection to ensure it's properly in place.&#x20;

#### Powering On

Plug in the power cord. The unit will begin sending current to the components. In most cases, you must then press and hold the ⏻ power button for several seconds to power on the phones. The Cellhasher can take up to 1 minute thereafter.

{% hint style="info" %}
After booting, you may need to toggle between OTG and USB modes if it doesn't boot in your desired mode. Toggle by double click the ⏻ power button. See more in [connection-modes-wi-fi-and-ethernet.md](../additional-docs/connection-modes-wi-fi-and-ethernet.md "mention")
{% endhint %}

That's it! Proceed to downloading Cellhasher Control in [set-up-download.md](../cellhasher-control/set-up-download.md "mention")
{% endhint %}
